Data Deletion Request

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Tarora is designed to respect your privacy. The app does not require you to create an account, and we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

What Data Does Tarora Store?

  • Reading history — Stored locally on your device only.
  • App preferences — Stored locally on your device only.
  • No account, no server-side data — We have no user accounts, profiles, or databases.

Because all data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the application. No data exists on our end to delete.

Data Shared with Third Parties

When you request a tarot reading, the card details and spread context are sent to third-party AI API providers (Groq, DeepSeek, and/or OpenAI) solely to generate the interpretation. These providers may retain anonymized usage data under their own policies.

To request deletion of any data held by these third parties, please contact them directly:

  • Groq — Privacy Policy & Data Request
  • DeepSeek — Privacy Policy & Data Request
  • OpenAI — Privacy Policy & Data Request

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Delete Local App Data (Recommended)

  1. Open your device's Settings app.
  2. Go to Apps or Application Manager.
  3. Tap on Tarora.
  4. Tap Storage & cache.
  5. Tap Clear storage or Delete data.

This will erase all local data, including reading history and preferences.

Option 2: Uninstall the App

  1. Long-press the Tarora icon on your home screen or app drawer.
  2. Tap Uninstall.
  3. Confirm to remove the app and all associated local data.

Option 3: Request Deletion via Email

If you believe any personal data has been retained on our end or if you have additional questions, send a deletion request to:

anarchyprometheus@gmail.com

Please include "Data Deletion Request — Tarora" in the subject line. We will respond within 30 days and confirm that any applicable data has been deleted.

Confirmation

Once you follow the steps above, all data associated with Tarora will be permanently deleted. No further action is required on your part.
