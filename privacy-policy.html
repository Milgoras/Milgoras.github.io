Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tarora ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information when you use the Tarora mobile application.

Information We Collect

Tarora does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers.

  • Reading history — Stored locally on your device only. Never sent to us.
  • Preferences — Stored locally on your device.
  • No account required — We do not ask for your name, email, or any personal details.

Third-Party Services

Tarora integrates the following third-party services, which may collect data subject to their own policies:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID), IP address, and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can learn more about how Google uses data at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

You may opt out of interest-based advertising via your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

AI Service Providers

Tarora uses third-party AI APIs (including Groq, DeepSeek, and OpenAI) to generate tarot reading interpretations. When you request a reading, the card details and spread context are sent to these providers solely for the purpose of generating the interpretation. These providers may retain anonymized usage data in accordance with their privacy policies:

  • Groq Privacy Policy
  • DeepSeek Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

Data Security

All communication with third-party AI APIs is conducted over HTTPS. No personal user data is stored on remote servers by us.

Children's Privacy

Tarora does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page.
